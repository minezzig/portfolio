about

I'm Greg, an international educator turned software developer with a passion for food. My curiosity for life's experiences has taken me across the globe; riding motorbikes in Asia 🛵, chilling with penguins in Antarctica 🇦🇶, and caring for majestic jaguars in the Amazon 🐆. Along the way, I've learned Spanish, become a certified scuba diver, and continued to indulge in my love for plant-based cuisine 🌱.


My journey hasn't just been about exploration — it's been about adaptation and learning. Living in diverse cultures has taught me how to quickly absorb new information and adapt to different environments. These skills have been evident in my teaching career, where I transitioned seamlessly to communicate, educate, and deliver effectively.


My love of development began early, creating websites to share my passions and hobbies. Fast forward years later, with a bootcamp under my belt, my enthusiasm for crafting designs and apps for the web persists. Powered by React, JavaScript, CSS and Tailwind.


When I'm not coding, you'll find me walking endless miles around a new city 🚶🏼‍♂️, soaking up the sun ☀️, or cycling through the concrete jungle 🚲.
