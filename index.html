about

I'm Greg, an anthropologist turned web developer with a passion for food. From New York to Spain, a curiosity for life's experiences has taken me across the globe; riding motorbikes in Asia 🛵, chilling with penguins in Antarctica 🇦🇶, and caring for majestic jaguars in the Amazon 🐆. Along the way, I've learned Spanish, become a certified scuba diver, and continued to indulge in my love for plant-based cuisine 🌱.


My journey hasn't just been about exploration—it's been about adaptation and learning. Living in a variety of languages and cultures has taught me how to quickly absorb new information and adapt to different environments. This skill has been evident in my teaching career, where I seamlessly transitioned to effectively communicate, educate, and deliver.


When I'm not coding, you'll find me walking endless miles around a new city 🚶🏼‍♂️, soaking up the sun ☀️, or cycling through the concrete jungle of Madrid 🚲.
